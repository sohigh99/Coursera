Relationship between Interop tables F4101Z1, F4101Z1A and Master tables F4101 and F4102 in R4101Z1I

Purpose of Document

The purpose of this document is to describe how to map columns from F4101Z1 and F4101Z1A by indicating columns from F4101 and F4102, and the additional validation routine involving related business functions. This document can be used to understand the relationship between F4101 and F4102 and the item characteristics stored in them.
